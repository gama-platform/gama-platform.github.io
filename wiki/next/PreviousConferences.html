Session 1 - Technological Integration and Tool Development​

  • COMMOD: Companion Modelling - Christophe Lepage

  • Usage of ABM in the use case of CSL@Guadalajara - Gamaliel Palomo, Arnaud Grignard, Alejandro Lepe, Mayra Gamboa

Session 3 - Environmental and Ecological Modeling​

  • MAELIA: a multi-agent platform for integrated modelling and evaluation of agricultural and bioeconomy systems - Manon Dardonville, Jean Villerd, Olivier Thérond

  • Coupling GAMA, R software and the OpenMOLE platform to explore an agent-based model simulating interactions between farms - Aurélien Peter, Romain Reuillon, Myriam Grillot

  • Metabolic modelling of the human gut microbiome: framing the problem to use GAMA - Eugenio Belda-Cuesta, Jean-Daniel Zucker, Edi Prifti
  • Probabilistic tsunami evacuation modelling for City of Catania, Eastern Sicily" - Saeed Soltani, Fatemeh Jalayer
  • Playground Wars -Territorial conflict simulation model - Ewa Tokarska-Gołębiewska, Maciej Szarejko
  • Proxemic stress as a hidden driver of physical inactivity in urban green areas – modelling and simulation of environmental interactions with a focus on the physiology of adrenaline-cortisol response - Maciej Szarejko, Przemysław Nowakowski, Szymon Dziedzic
