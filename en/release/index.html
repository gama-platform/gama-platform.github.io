Dear GAMA Modellers,

GAMA 1.8.1 is here !

We are excited to announce our latest release: GAMA 1.8.1. It is available for download right away! We have introduced many new features requested by our users and a redesigned website with documentation for both users and developers.

We welcome and encourage you to download and experience all these exciting new features.

The GAMA Platform team
